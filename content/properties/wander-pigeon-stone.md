## Activities & Attractions

### Dollywood
**Theme Park** • 5.2 miles away

Experience world-class roller coasters, award-winning shows, and authentic Smoky Mountain crafts at Dolly Parton's flagship theme park. Seasonal festivals and new attractions keep families coming back year after year.

### Great Smoky Mountains National Park
**Nature** • 12 miles away

Explore America's most visited national park with over 800 miles of hiking trails, stunning waterfalls, and abundant wildlife. From easy nature walks to challenging mountain climbs, adventure awaits at every turn.

### Ripley's Aquarium of the Smokies
**Aquarium** • 4.8 miles away

Walk through the underwater tunnel surrounded by sharks, rays, and tropical fish. Interactive touch tanks and daily dive shows make this a must-visit attraction for all ages.

### TITANIC Museum
**Museum** • 4.2 miles away

Step aboard a half-scale replica of the famous ship and experience the tragic voyage through interactive exhibits. Authentic artifacts and personal stories bring history to life.

### Anakeesta
**Adventure Park** • 5.1 miles away

Soar through the treetops on zip lines, stroll across scenic skywalks, and explore mountain gardens. This mountaintop adventure park offers thrills and relaxation in equal measure.

### The Island in Pigeon Forge
**Entertainment Complex** • 4.6 miles away

Ride the Great Smoky Mountain Wheel for panoramic views, browse unique shops, and enjoy live music at this vibrant entertainment destination. The dancing fountains provide evening entertainment.

### Hatfield & McCoy Dinner Feud
**Dinner Show** • 4.9 miles away

Laugh until your sides hurt at this hilarious singing and dancing competition between America's most famous feuding families. The all-you-can-eat Southern feast adds to the fun.

### Ober Gatlinburg
**Ski Resort & Amusement Park** • 8.3 miles away

Ski and snowboard in winter, or enjoy year-round activities including an alpine slide, scenic chairlift rides, and wildlife encounters. The aerial tramway offers breathtaking mountain views.
